The Meta Ads Strategy That Generated $2M for Our Clients

Breaking down the exact framework our media buyers use to achieve consistent 4-6x ROAS for e-commerce and service businesses.

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram) remain the most powerful customer acquisition engine in the world—if you know how to talk to the algorithm. In 2024, we managed over $500k in ad spend, yielding over $2M in direct revenue.

Phase 1: Creative-Led Strategy

The biggest mistake brands make is relying on "hacks" in the Ads Manager. In 2025, your creative is your targeting. We focus on:

  • UGC (User Generated Content): Authentic videos that don't look like ads.
  • Benefit-Driven Statics: Clean, high-impact visuals that solve a problem instantly.
  • The Hook: Grabbing attention in the first 0.5 seconds.

Phase 2: Broad Targeting

We've found that narrowing your audience too much actually hurts performance. By using broad targeting with high-quality creative, we let Meta's AI find the buyers for us.

Phase 3: Relentless Testing

We test 10-20 new creative concepts every week. Winners get scaled; losers are cut immediately. This high-velocity testing is the secret to sustained growth.
