FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a brand identity?
+
A complete brand identity typically takes 7–14 business days depending on scope and revision rounds. Rush projects can be accommodated at an additional fee. We'll give you a precise timeline in your custom proposal.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely. We work with brands across 50+ countries and all services are available to international clients. We work across time zones and use collaborative tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of your location.
What is the minimum budget to start?
+
We have packages for every stage of business — starting from $499/month for our Starter Brand package. Book a free call and we'll recommend the best option for your budget and goals. We never oversell — we only recommend what you need.
Do you offer one-time projects or ongoing retainers?
+
Both. We offer one-time brand projects (like a logo or website) and ongoing monthly marketing retainers. We also offer hybrid arrangements — for example, a one-time brand identity package with an optional ongoing social media retainer added later.
How do I get started with Brandozer?
+
Simply fill out the contact form above or WhatsApp us directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute strategy call where our experts will listen to your goals and walk you through exactly how we can help. No commitment required.
What makes Brandozer different from other marketing agencies?
+
Three things: 1) We're a full-service agency — everything under one roof with one unified brand strategy. 2) We're results-obsessed — every decision is tied to measurable outcomes. 3) We treat every client like our only client — dedicated teams, transparent reporting, and genuine care about your success.
